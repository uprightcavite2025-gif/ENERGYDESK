🎡 Brain Break 04 · Movement Reset

Movement Wheel

Spin the wheel and complete one movement prompt. This short break helps reset your posture, body, and energy before continuing the course.

How to play

Press spin, wait for the wheel to stop, then complete the single movement prompt shown below the wheel.

🧍
Posture and body reset Stand, stretch, or correct your sitting position.
⚡
Energy movement Wake up your body with simple movement.
👀
Screen break Give your eyes and face a short reset.
!
Move safely Choose a safe version of the movement for your space. Stop if anything feels uncomfortable.
